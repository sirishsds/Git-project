Jaya Multiple Campus is located in Jorpati, Kathmandu. The college offers BBS and BA/BSW program in affiliation with Tribhuvan University, Nepal.  

The campus was established in 2064 B.S. The campus is purely a non-profit co-educational institution which has adopted a policy to provide secondary, Bachelor and Master level education at moderate expense with the aim to cover every socio-economic class student in and around the community.
